Getting Around in
College Park, MD

Helpful Tips for Getting Around

Use public transportation: The University of Maryland operates a shuttle bus system that connects the campus with surrounding areas, including the College Park Metro Station. This is a convenient and affordable way to get around. Walk or bike: College Park is a compact and walkable city, with bike lanes and pedestrian-friendly sidewalks. Walking or biking is a great way to get around, especially for short trips. Use ridesharing services: Ridesharing services like Uber and Lyft are widely available in College Park and can be a convenient way to get around, especially for longer trips or late-night travel. Park strategically: Parking can be limited in College Park, especially near campus. Consider parking in a nearby lot or garage and walking to your destination. Take advantage of campus resources: The University of Maryland offers a variety of resources and services to help students get around campus and the surrounding area, including a campus shuttle, bike-share program, and car-sharing program. Plan ahead: Be aware of any special events or construction that may affect your travel plans and allow extra time to get around during these periods. Know your route: Familiarize yourself with the streets and transportation options available in College Park to help you get around with confidence.
